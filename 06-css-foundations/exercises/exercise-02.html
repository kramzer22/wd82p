From Garden to Plate

A potager is a French term to an ornament vegetable or kitchen garden. Often flowers (editble and non-edible) and herbs are planted with the vegetables to enhance the garden' beauty. The goal is to make the function of providing food aesthetically pleasing.

What to Plant

Plants are chosen as much for their functionality as for color and form. Many are trained to grow upward. A well-designed potager can provide food, cut flowers and herbs for the home with very little maintenance. Potagers can disguise their functionality of providing for a home in a wide array of forms - from the carefree style of the cottage garden to the formality of a knot garden.
