THE HENRY RESORT

The Henry Resort Taramindu Laiya features an outdoor swimming pool, garden, a shared lounge and restaurant in Batangas City. Among the facilities at this property are a 24-hour front desk and a concierge service, along with free WiFi throughout the property. Guests can make use of a bar.

WHAT TO EXPECT?

All units in the resort are fitted with a coffee machine. With a private bathroom equipped with a bidet and free toiletries, rooms at The Henry Resort Taramindu Laiya also offer pool view. At the accommodation, each room includes air conditioning and a safety deposit box.

Breakfast is available daily, and includes continental, American and Asian options.

The Henry Resort Taramindu Laiya offers a sun terrace.

The nearest airport is Ninoy Aquino International Airport, 127 km from the resort.
